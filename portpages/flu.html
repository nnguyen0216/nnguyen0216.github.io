Flu Vaccination Promotions Poster


My first ever project at my first visual design position.

The Process: The UCSD Health Promotion Services (HPS) center wanted to advertise a free* flu vaccine clinic event to students on campus. This year, the HPS director requested a warm and inviting social media post (last year's was not a welcoming poster as it contained needles) and allowed me freedom in devising mock ups for the promotion piece.


Color played an important role in the welcoming aspect of this project. I chose warmer-toned pinks and nude tones in order to maintain a softer appearance, so that students would feel welcomed when encountering the post on their feeds. To garner attention, I chose a blue color for the *most* important information, ensuring that students wouldn't miss it.
