YouTube Redesign

Improving the usability of features on landscape orientation.
This case study was done on YouTube’s mobile app version 13.50.52 on iOS!

Team members

Winson Dieu, Lauren Choy, Madeline Ng, Evon Hung, Kyoko Ishikawa

Timeline of case study

November - December 2018 (3 weeks)

The Problem

This case study focuses on mitigating pain points found while watching videos in landscape mode in order to make the experience more intuitive and less obtrusive for the user.

Evaluation

We conducted the app evaluation on our phones. Some of us used the app daily, while others did not even have it downloaded on their phones. Since we all had varying levels of familiarity with the app, it allowed us to discover what we should really focus on. For both new and frequent users of the app, it was agreed that landscape mode was not very intuitive to use.
